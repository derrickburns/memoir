## THE CHARACTER INTRODUCTION FORMULA

For EVERY character's first appearance, include:

### Required Elements

1. **Full Name** (first + last)
   - Exception: Young children, pets, or characters known only by first name with explicit note

2. **Physical Description**
   - Age or age range
   - Height/build (for significant characters)
   - Distinguishing features
   - How they dress/carry themselves

3. **Race/Ethnicity**
   - CRITICAL in memoir about racial identity
   - State explicitly, don't leave to assumption
   - June Cross repeatedly asks "I'm assuming...?"

4. **Relationship to Narrator**
   - Friend, family, colleague, mentor, etc.
   - How they met (if relevant)
   - Why they matter to the story

5. **Context**
   - Who they are in the world
   - Their role/occupation
   - What makes them significant

6. **Voice/Manner** (for major characters)
   - How they speak
   - Personality traits
   - Behavioral characteristics
