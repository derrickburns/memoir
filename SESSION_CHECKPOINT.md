## Key Principles for This Work

### Structural:
- **Chronological interweaving** - cut between threads naturally
- **Subtle irony** - reader discovers synchronicity (don't over-explain)
- **Show, don't tell** - concrete scenes, not summaries
- **"The reader should discover it like I did!"** - Derrick's directive

### Voice:
- **Direct, not evasive**
- **Concrete, not abstract**
- **Raw, not polished**
- **Scene-based, not summary**

### Quality Checklist (Every Chapter):
- [ ] Characters introduced with detail at first mention
- [ ] Time markers clear (elapsed time, not just dates)
- [ ] Important moments expanded (not dismissed in 3-4 paragraphs)
- [ ] Concrete details shown (not abstract emotions named)
- [ ] Through-lines advancing
